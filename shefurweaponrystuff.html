Weaponry - Main -

SheFurrs have four arms, which makes them extremely dangerous in combat. An typical configuration: two main arms, melee weapons + two smaller arms, light firearms. This allows SheFur to fight in melee and ranged combat simultaneously. Weapon depends on: hive size, hive tactics, hive culture, available materials, caste specialization. SheFurrs can fight: with two melee weapons, with melee + firearms, with four weapons at once. This makes them terrifying fighters. All of firearms shots by slightly arc traectories (thats logical because of physics).

IMPORTANT - All of SheFur weapon are not another living organisms, they are hand crafted by SheFurrs with materials.

Who does not carry weapons: nymphs (workers), nurses, youngers, queens. They only take weapons in rare emergencies. Not the queen.

Safety & Friendly Logic -

SheFurr's weapons does not affect them selfs. It means that if both SheFur and firearm is from one hive then: ricochets never hit hive members, symbionts are dangerous to SheFurrs but less than to outsiders, venom will not work by hive membership, only on outsiders. Weapons require SheFurrs resonance by hive membership, outsiders & other SheFurrs of other hives cannot use them. Also outside SheFurrs can steel or equip a weapon from each other hives, but then SheFurrs need to change/optimize this weapons for gaining new hive membership, Only works with SheFurrs species by race not other species. IMPORTANT - that SheFurrs instead of hive membership rule, can harm them selfs by their own weaponry.

Resonance hive membership Link -

All SheFur firearms have their own little antennae like SheFurr's antennae. The coolest part is that SheFur firearms can establish a direct resonance link with the individual carrying them, but only at close ranges. This allows the SheFur to literally feel the firearm as a part of their own body and coordinate and control it directly, which makes SheFur combat even more dangerous.

Firearms -

SheFur firearms incorporate: biolumen components, biolumen ammunition, psyonic-linked organic parts. The weapons glow faintly, especially in darkness.

Ammunition Type :

SheFur guns do not fire bullets. They fire: Triangular needle-spikes, long, sharp, aerodynamic, partially organic. These spikes leave a bright orange biolumen trail behind them.

Firing Modes -

Every firearm has several modes:

Standard Mode :

One trigger pull, one shot, precise, quiet, efficient.

Rapid-Fire Mode :

Hold the trigger, continuous fire, stops only when ammunition is depleted.

Impulse Mode :

Spikes become impact-amplified, create a powerful shockwave on hit. Ideal for: breaking armors, breaking materials, stunning targets. The firing rate in this mode are slowly.

Firearms Behavior -

SheFur firearms are: silent (not at all but still very silent because no powder & exploding, It sounds like something biological), capable of ricocheting spike bullets at angles. Equipped with two small antennaes like SheFurr's own, these allow the weapon to function only when held by a hive member SheFur so prevents enemy use. And firearms have little homing, not like cheat, only very slightlly.

IMPORTANT - Ricochet Logic: spike bullets can bounce off surfaces, but only when the bullets not hited enemy but hited walls then works ricocheting with small homing. Never ricochets toward his same hive members.

Reloading & Overheating -

Firearms have: reload cycles, overheat thresholds, recoil. All vary by hive, caste, and weapon type. Its created for balance of SheWorld.

Environmental Performance -

SheFur weapons work: underwater & in liquids, in cold, but slowlly. In extreme cold, weapons enter anabiosis, unless inside a hive. They do not works good in vacuum. Underwater and in liquids: weapons still fire but spikes travel slower.

Weaponry - Colds -

General :

SheFur use cold weapons primarily with their two main arms. Using them in the smaller arms is possible but rare because: main arms provide better strength, better precision, better control. Cold weapons are often coated with SheFurr's paralytic venom, applied through their saliva.

1. SheFur Sword -

Design :

Handle wrapped in silk, blade made of Dread Metal, dark in color, long. With a unique curvature: slight forward curve near the base, strong backward curve near the tip.

Features :

Front edge has sharp hooks and tooth-like serrations, excellent at gripping and holding onto targets, designed for tearing and controlled cutting.

Use :

Can be dual-wielded (one sword in each main hands).

2. SheFur Spear -

A versatile mid-range weapon.

Design :

Medium length, handle wrapped in silk, rear end has a small spike, front end has a long sharp Dread Metal spike, spike includes serrated teeth for improved grip.

Throwing System :

The spear attaches to a special vessel strapped to the SheFurr's arm: Allows the spear to be thrown and automatically pulled back. Effective range: up to 12 meters.

Ricochet Guidance :

The spear has limited angle-based redirection: if thrown at a wall it can bounce at an angle, often redirects toward the intended target.

Use :

Cannot be dual-wielded only in one main hand. Usually carried on the back as secondary weapon.

3. SheFur Daggers -

Small, fast.

Design :

Small triangular blades, perfectly straight and sharp, handle wrapped in silk, made from solid Dread Metal, dark in color.

Use :

Can be thrown (no return mechanism), can be used in every hand, extremely fast and precise, usually carried at the waist as backup weapons.

4. SheFur Whip -

A flexible, segmented weapon.

Design :

Handle wrapped in silk, central elastic spine made of cartilage, along the spine many sharp segmented blades, segments made of Dread Metal, segments include hooks for gripping.

Behavior :

Extremely elastic. Designed to: wrap around targets, restrain, apply controlled pressure. Can automatically coil and tighten. Very fast and difficult to evade.

Use :

Usually carried at the waist as secondary weapon, can be holden only in one main hand.

5. SheFur Chakrams -

The most beloved weapon of SheFurrs.

Design :

Circular weapon, handle area wrapped in silk, central open hole, made of Dread Metal, dark in color. Blades: outer ring sharp hooked blades, inner ring small sharp blades.

Combat Role :

Chakrams are ideal for: close combat, mid-range combat, throwing attacks.

Throwing Behavior :

Thrown like a boomerang - returns to the user. Can ricochet off surfaces, can angle-target toward enemies, very fast, highly versatile.

Use :

Worn at the waist. Can be used only in pairs by one chakram per main hands or second hands or both pairs by main & second hands.

Weaponry - Firearms -

Light Weapons -

Light weapons are designed for the two smaller arms allowing SheFurrs to combine melee and ranged combat at the same time. They are compact, fast, and optimized for close-to-mid-range engagements.

1. SheFur Pistol -

General Description :

A medium-sized firearm. Handle wrapped in silk. Handle is uneven, organic in shape. Constructed from: cartilage, leather-like material. Has a smell antennae for resonance connection with host.

Combat Role :

Effective at close and mid-range.

Use :

Commonly carried in both smaller arms, usually two pistols at once. The standard pistol is the most universal light weapon in the hive.

2. SheFur Elite Pistol -

General Description :

Also a light-sized firearm, but heavier and more powerful than common SheFur pistol. Handle wrapped in silk. Looks like the standard pistol but: slightly larger, with a longer barrel, reinforced with more Dread Metal. Has a medium more elite-like antennae for resonance connection with host.

Abilities :

The elite pistol can: pierce straight through a target, and then the spike bullet ricochets and continues toward another enemies. This makes it extremely effective in dense combat or against armored opponents.

Use :

Typically carried in both smaller arms, two pistols total. Preferred by elite scouts / elite warriors - Elites.

Medium Weapons -

1. SheFur SMG -

General Description :

A lightweight, medium-sized bio-firearm used for rapid close-to-mid-range combat. Handle wrapped in silk, uneven, small stock for stability, three rotating growth-like structures around the barrel, these rotate during firing, part of the weapon's mechanism. Has a medium antennae for resonance connection with host.

Use :

Fast firing, can be mounted on any arm, main or secondary. Maximum up to four SMGs at once (two main arms + two smaller arms).

2. SheFur Rifle -

General Description :

A lightweight, medium-length rifle designed for precision and penetration. Handle wrapped in silk, small stock, two long barrels like spreaded two parts. Built-in aiming structures (no optical sights needed, SheFurr's vision is superior). Has a medium antennae for resonance connection with host.

Abilities :

Fires strengthened needle-spike bullets, capable to penetrate through targets, medium firing speed. Does not have Rapid-Fire mode.

Use :

Can only be used on main arms, up to two rifles (one per main arm).

3. SheFur Assault Rifle -

Core weapon of SheFurr's medium firearms.

General Description :

A medium-sized weapon that attaches directly to the SheFurr's arm, becoming like a part of the limb. No traditional handle, no stock, long structure extending almost to the elbow. Barrel formed by two jaw-like growths: upper and lower, with hook-like teeth, the jaw opens wider the longer the weapon fires. Has a medium antennae for resonance connection with host.

Abilities :

Medium firing speed, strengthened ammunition. Special charge mode: weapon stores bullets, then releases all the entire magazine extremely fast.

Use :

Only on main arms, up to two assault weapons (one per main arm).

4. SheFur Shock Assault Rifle (Experimental) -

General Description :

An experimental version of the standard assault rifle. Blue and cyan coloration, attaches to the arm, similar jaw-barrel structure. Has a medium blue antennae for resonance connection with host.

Abilities :

Fires electric energy clusters instead of spike bullets, medium firing speed. Can charge energy and release a short lightning-like discharge. If misused, the electrical discharge can shock the wielder.

Use :

Only on main arms, up to two shock weapons (per one by main hands).

5. SheFur Elite Assault Rifle -

General Description :

The elite version of the standard assault rifle. Slightly larger, reinforced with Dread Metal plates, more ornate and aggressive design. Has a medium more elite-like antennae for resonance connection with host.

Abilities :

Fires like the standard assault weapon. Have additional psyonic wave mode: spike bullet emit psyonic shockwaves. Affects enemy cognition, causes disorientation, confusion, or mental disruption. Does not strenghtlly affect SheFurrs of the same hive by hive membership. Affects SheFurrs from other hives much more strongly. The wielder briefly loses orientation for a moment after firing (only a second, not dangerous).

Use :

Only on main arms, up to two elite assault weapons (per one by main hands).

Clothing & Armor -

SheFur clothing: does not depend on winter or summer, is entirely based on personal preference, is optional, most individuals remove everything during rest. Never covers the feet because of digitigrade but may cover the legs. Combat castes must wear anything protective even if minimal. Eggs, larvae, and cocoons never wear clothing because they need to develop by stages in egg/combs/cocoons. Clothing is cultural, functional, and caste-specific.

nurses and nymphs (workers) basiclly do not wear clothing. but they can sometimes wear the base clothing: ragged silk strips made from silk and leftover cocoon material, full of holes, tears, and uneven edges, usually dirty-white/light orange. This is the most common everyday clothing inside the hive like underwear for all individuals.

Clothing of the Arts (Combat Styles) -

These are the three major arts of combat clothing, depending on the chosen fighting style.

Stealth/Agility Clothing -

Worn by: scouts, warriors, and all who love Lightness combat. Characteristics: open, flexible, fast, lightweight, usually dark, matching hive and SheFurrs coloration. Materials: leather, shed skin, silk, but mostly leather. Tight-fitting, torn, uneven, asymmetrical. Often includes a mask covering the muzzle.

Medium Armor Clothing -

Worn by: combat individuals who choose a balance between agility and armor. Characteristics: open but heavier than Lightness. Dark colors matching hive palette. Materials: Dread Metal, leather, leather is torn and uneven, silk. Armor is: light, sharp, complex in shape, tight-fitting. A hybrid of protection and mobility.

Heavy Armor Clothing -

Worn by: individuals who choose full armor and defense, mostly heavy likes. Characteristics: open but heavy. Dark colors. Material: Dread Metal mostly. Armor consists of: many plates, sharp edges, complex forms. Often includes: Dread Metal shield also sharp, sharp Dread Metal helmet. This is the most protective combat attire.

Elite Clothing -

Elite individuals wear the same three varients. But with elite enhancements: more serious refined designs, armor with scar-patterns, improved materials. Dread Metal glows with bright orange psyonic biolumen symbols.

Royal's Clothing -

Princesses are exceptional and have unique attire. Their clothing is: smooth, not torn. made from: silk, leather, specially crafted hive fabric. The fabric shimmers because it contains hive biolumen, gentle, light, elegant. Princess clothing is the most refined in the hive.

The Queen does not wear clothing. She has no need for it.

Stuff & Other -

1. Dread Metal -

Dread Metal is a hybrid material created by the hive. It is a mixture of: biological components, complex extremely durable materials and a small amount of the psyonic biolumen. Its appearance: dark gray or dark, sharp rigid texture, sometimes contains embedded speckles, covered in bright orange psyonic biolumen patterns. These patterns form a symbolic language of the hive, completely incomprehensible to outsiders.

Dread Metal is: super-durable, elastic (can flex without breaking), intimidating in appearance, psyonically resonant with hive, self-repairing. If damaged, the metal: slowly regrows, restores its shapes. This regeneration is slow but reliable.

Growth :

Dread Metal can grow and expand but: grows very slowly, it grows upward forming spikes, towers, or jagged protrusions. It requires a special symbiont-based fluid to begin growth, this fluid is regurgitated by SheFurr's individuals, the fluid is a trigger that activates the growth process. Without this fluid, the metal will not alive in world. Dread Metal is symbiosis of SheFurrs and some symbionts.

Use :

Dread Metal is used for: walls, towers, some armor & heavy armor, some weapons, some tools, some traps, structural supports & hive infrastructure. It is one of the most valuable materials on the planet created by symbiosis & SheFurrs.

For other species, Dread Metal is considered extremely precious.

2. SheFur Fungus -

SheFurr's fungus is grown in special chambers inside the hive. The fungus grows exclusively on SheFurr's feces, because of symbiosis: feces provide a trigger for growth, the fungus cannot reproduce or spread without this substrate. This fungus exists only inside SheFur hives. The fungus must begin its growth in feces. After the initial stage, it can continue growing without further feeding. In large farms the fungus can: grow upward, break through the hive's outer layers and form external fungal hills visible from outside the hive.

The fungus looks like: mass of whitish organic tissue, small embedded speckles, faint white or purple weak biolumen barely noticeable unless in darkness.

Use :

The fungus is a main food resource of SheFurrs and hives.

3. Bright Thingy -

Appearance :

Bright Thingy is a bright orange-yellow glass-like material, similar to amber and ice, with a faint yellow biolumen. It is transparent, very strong, slightly elastic, and contains sandy and dark-orange speckles. It can be dyed with pigments to gain different colors.

Formation :

Bright Thingy appears after a larva fully forms and breaks its egg, exposing the embryonic fluid to the environment. This makes it a by-product resource. The fluid hardens over time into a glass analogue. Cold speeds up hardening but makes the material more fragile. Full hardening takes about 2 days (SheWorld time).

Liquid State Behavior :

In liquid form, Bright Thingy behaves like Earth water: it hardens only on the outer surface, forming a strong elastic crust while the inside remains liquid. The crust is transparent and resembles a slime-like with an amber / ice appearance. Over time, the crust thickens, allowing SheFurr to cut and collect solid pieces.

Use :

Bright Thingy is used only for: fun, decoration, and creativity. It has no practical use and is not used in construction. But some very specific hives / colonies can use this thing.
